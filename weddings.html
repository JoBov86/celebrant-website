Weddings

I offer a wide range of wedding ceremonies and vow renewals, from the traditional to the more unusual, including Wicca and woodland mediaeval. I create ceremonies that truly fit each couple and can offer ways of involving family members, guests, children and even family pets, as desired. These include such mini-ceremonies as broom jumping, hand fasting, cakes and ale, oathing stones, ring blessings (or warmings), mixing sands, loving cups, wishing trees and tree planting, unity candles, cocktails-mixing or tea ceremonies.

Venues and Themes...

I am happy to work in any type of venue, indoors or out, and to offer ceremonies that reflect and fit with the unique aspects of the place and of the couple’s relationship, including themed weddings.
Essentially, each is created with the couple to make their wedding day a truly unique celebration and reflection of their love and of their union.

Please note that, In England, a celebrant cannot legally marry a couple, so they would need either to marry separately at a registry office, prior to the ceremony, or the registrars may be willing to attend a celebrant run ceremony to fulfil the legal part of the marriage.
